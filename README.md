# Project Portfolio: Just-In-Time (JIT) Inventory Optimization
**Role:** Inventory Manager / Project Manager  
**Project Type:** Operational Transformation (Supply Chain & Inventory)

---

## 1. Project Charter

### Project Purpose
The facility is currently holding 45 days of excess raw material inventory, tying up operational cash flow and resulting in a 4% annual material wastage due to shelf-life expiration. This project introduces a Just-In-Time (JIT) framework to align supplier deliveries directly with weekly manufacturing schedules.

### Objectives & Success Metrics
* **Goal 1:** Reduce average days of inventory on hand from 45 days to 14 days.
* **Goal 2:** Drop material waste/expiration rates to 0.5% or lower.
* **Success Metric:** Free up $50,000 in tied-up operational capital within 30 days of implementation.

### Scope
* **In Scope:** Re-negotiating delivery schedules with top 3 raw material vendors, setting new minimum/maximum stock thresholds, establishing automated reorder points.
* **Out of Scope:** Changing downstream customer distribution logistics or sourcing entirely new suppliers.

---

## 2. Project Plan (High-Level Timeline)

* **Phase 1: Data Analysis & Baselining (Week 1)**
  * Analyze past 12 months of production data to find true material usage patterns.
  * Identify top 3 high-volume, high-cost raw materials to target first.
* **Phase 2: Supplier Alignment & SLAs (Week 2)**
  * Meet with key vendors to agree on smaller, more frequent delivery windows.
  * Establish Service Level Agreements (SLAs) for strict delivery punctuality.
* **Phase 3: Implementation & Training (Weeks 3-4)**
  * Program new reorder points into the inventory tracking database.
  * Train the purchasing and receiving teams on the new automated reorder triggers.

## 4. Risk Assessment

| Risk Identified | Impact Level | Mitigation Strategy (Prevention Plan) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Supplier Delay:** A vendor misses a delivery window, risking a production halt. | Critical | Maintain a mandatory 3-day "Safety Buffer Stock" on-site for emergency use. |
| **Data Inaccuracy:** Software stock counts don't match physical floor counts. | High | Conduct a mandatory physical inventory count cycle-audit before going live. |
